## Lead section *(no heading on Wikipedia)*

**Vegam Solutions** (*Vegam* from Sanskrit वेगं, pronounced [ʋeː.ɡɐ̃]; *VAY-gum*, meaning "speed" or "velocity") is a Singapore-headquartered industrial software company founded in January 2000 by Subramanyam Kasibhat and Savita Kasibhat.[^12] The company operated as Aureole Technologies Private Limited in Bengaluru, India, until a Singapore entity was established in 2015; the original Indian entity was rebranded under the Vegam Solutions name in 2022.[^12] The company develops a manufacturing operations management platform used by manufacturers in process and discrete industries.[^2]

Vegam's principal engineering and delivery operations are in India. Its product portfolio includes the AI Intime, Vegam Robotics, and Vegam SFS platforms.

The company's customers in process manufacturing have included BASF, Henkel,[^2][^3] and the MBCC Group — now part of Sika AG — where its overall equipment effectiveness module was deployed across more than twenty production lines.[^4]

## 1. History

### 1.1 Founding (2000–2010)

The company was founded in January 2000 by Subramanyam Kasibhat and Savita Kasibhat, and operated as **Aureole Technologies Private Limited**, based in Bengaluru, India.[^12] Its earliest work was as a software and hardware solutions provider for manufacturing customers, including products marketed at the time as i-PAS, an SAP mobility enabler for manufacturing plants deployed in Australia, China, and Malaysia, and an early manufacturing execution system used to rank factory performance.[^12]

According to the company's founder, the business spent approximately seven years working closely with a single early customer — described in independent reporting as a German manufacturer — before rewriting the software into a multi-tenant product offering.[^13]

### 1.2 Transition to a product company (2011–2019)

During this period, the company shifted its focus from services delivery to platform development.[^12] In August 2015, a Singapore-incorporated entity, Vegam Solutions Pte Ltd, was established, and the company began marketing itself as an Industry 4.0 enabler offering platforms and modules for smart factories.[^12] The Bengaluru entity, Aureole Technologies Private Limited, continued to operate alongside the Singapore parent until September 2022, when it was rebranded under the Vegam Solutions name.[^12]

The company has remained privately held and bootstrapped throughout this period.[^13]

### 1.3 Multinational deployments (2020–2024)

From 2020, Vegam's engagement with Henkel Adhesive Technologies — which operates more than 130 manufacturing sites worldwide — was the subject of features in *Manufacturing Digital*, *Technology Magazine*, and *Business Chief UK & Europe*.[^2][^3][^6] In a December 2020 profile of Henkel Adhesive Technologies, the company's then–Head of Digital Operations, Dr Nick Miesen, characterised the Vegam system as "a powerful tool from the shop floor to the board room."[^7]

In early 2022, Vegam was selected by the MBCC Group — a construction-chemicals manufacturer subsequently acquired by Sika AG — to deploy its overall equipment effectiveness module across more than twenty production lines internationally, on a software-as-a-service commercial model. The engagement was reported to have reached commercial amortisation in under twelve months.[^4]

### 1.4 Corporate restructuring and GenAI evolution (2024–2025)

In April 2024, a related Indian operating entity, **Vegam Smart Factory Solutions Private Limited**, was registered in Bangalore.[^14] As part of a broader move into generative-AI–driven manufacturing intelligence, the company launched **AI Intime** in January 2025, an enterprise agentic-AI platform extending the group's focus beyond conventional manufacturing-operations software. *(Open: independent source for AI Intime launch date; relationship between 2024 Indian entity, Singapore parent, and any US-incorporated affiliate is not visible in public filings.)*

## 2. Products

### 2.1 AI Intime

AI Intime is an enterprise agentic-AI platform developed by Vegam Solutions for deployment in on-premises and air-gapped environments. The product is positioned for organisations operating in regulated environments, including manufacturing, financial services, healthcare, defence, and research. *(Open: needs 1–2 sentences of factual product description from an independent source.)*

### 2.2 Vegam Robotics

Vegam Robotics is the company's robotics and hardware-automation product line. The company has indicated that the product is being developed through a process of identifying real-world manufacturing problems on the shop floor and prototyping robotic solutions in collaboration with its R&D teams. *(Open: needs independent description.)*

### 2.3 Vegam SFS

Vegam SFS is a manufacturing operations management platform for batch and process industries.[^4] It covers material intake, production execution, quality, and dispatch in a connected workflow system, and is configurable to support operations from manual recording through to fully automated, distributed-control-system-integrated production lines.[^4]

#### Modules

Vegam SFS is organised around five functional modules, corresponding to stages of the production workflow:

| Module | Function |
|---|---|
| Goods Receipt (GR) | Inbound material handling: truck registration and safety checks, unloading-bay routing, weighbridge integration, goods-receipt verification against purchase orders, and certificate-of-analysis processing. |
| Staging | Pre-production material preparation: warehouse picking, pre-weighing and kitting against bills of materials, and tracking of cut-open bags and partial consumption with full traceability. |
| Manufacturing | Production execution: digital standard operating procedures, recipe and batch management, real-time process-parameter capture from PLC and DCS systems, downtime and deviation handling, and OEE tracking. |
| Dispatch | Outbound operations: order grouping, packing and labelling (including GHS-compliant labels), shipment scheduling, and integration with enterprise systems for dispatch documentation. |
| LIMS | Laboratory information management: incoming-material inspection, in-process quality checks, final-product testing, certificate-of-analysis generation, and bi-directional integration with external laboratory systems. |

### 2.4 Legacy products

Earlier products that have been retired or consolidated into the current line include, in alphabetical order: *SFS Lite*; *vCMS*; *Vegam 4i*; *Vegam Label*; *vMaint4*; *vOEE*; *vProMax*; and *Zing*.[^2][^3][^4]

## 3. Operations

Vegam Solutions is headquartered in Singapore, and operates in India, Singapore, the United Arab Emirates, and the United States. The company's principal engineering and delivery centres in India are in Bengaluru and Hubli; the registered office of its Indian operating entity is at Spatium South Park, JP Nagar 3rd Phase, Bengaluru.[^14] As of 2025, the company reported approximately 180 employees, primarily based in India, and was generating annual revenue in the region of US$10 million; it has remained bootstrapped, with no outside venture funding.[^13]
